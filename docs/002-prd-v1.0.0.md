# Product Requirements Document v1.0.0

**Project:** Alpton Construction Website & Admin Portal
**Version:** v1.0.0
**Date:** 2026-03-28
**Status:** Defined

## Product Overview

The Alpton Construction Website & Admin Portal is a digital platform designed to showcase the company's construction portfolio, detail their services, and act as a primary lead generation engine. The core differentiator of the site is the "BUILD NOW, PAY LATER" inquiry system—a progressive wizard that qualifies potential clients for financing. Alongside the public-facing Single Page Application (SPA), a secure Admin Portal allows Alpton staff to review leads and manage portfolio entries with an automated image watermarking pipeline.

## User Personas

### Persona 1: Potential Client (Homeowner / Business Owner)
- **Role:** Individual or commercial entity looking for construction services.
- **Goals:** View past work to establish trust, understand offered services, and easily apply for construction financing.
- **Pain Points:** Unclear pricing, intimidating loan/financing applications, difficulty trusting contractors without visible proof of quality.
- **Technical Level:** Novice to Intermediate

### Persona 2: Internal Admin (Alpton Staff)
- **Role:** Sales representative, project manager, or marketing staff at Alpton Construction.
- **Goals:** Review qualified leads from the website, contact prospects, and maintain an up-to-date portfolio of past projects.
- **Pain Points:** Disorganized lead tracking (e.g., email inbox clutter), manual effort to watermark and upload project photos, lack of centralized data.
- **Technical Level:** Intermediate

## Success Criteria

- [ ] Successful deployment of the SPA with sub-2-second load times.
- [ ] 100% of uploaded portfolio images have the Alpton watermark securely applied.
- [ ] The "BUILD NOW, PAY LATER" wizard achieves a completion rate of >30% for started sessions.
- [ ] Admins can successfully log in and view submitted leads in a centralized dashboard.

## Out of Scope (v1.0.0)

- Integrated online payments for construction services.
- Client-facing project management / progress tracking portal.
- Complex CRM integrations (e.g., Salesforce); standard dashboard will suffice for MVP.

## Constraints & Assumptions

- **Constraint:** Portfolio images must not be accessible without a watermark (requires backend or edge-layer processing).
- **Constraint:** The SPA must be highly responsive to accommodate mobile users.
- **Assumption:** Alpton will provide the specific qualification questions for the "BUILD NOW, PAY LATER" flow.
- **Assumption:** A single admin role is sufficient for MVP (no complex RBAC).
